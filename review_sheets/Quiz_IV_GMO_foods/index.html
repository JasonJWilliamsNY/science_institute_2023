Quiz Review Sheet

Quiz: GMO Foods Lab

Ground rules/instructions

  1. No talking - During the quiz there is absolutely no talking. If you have a question, please raise your hand. If I can fairly answer your question (for example about a ambiguous statement or error) I will and will inform the other quiz takers.
  2. Spread out - you must have an empty chair to your right and left.
  3. You must bring your Chromebook - quiz will be online
  4. When the music stops you must submit your quiz - more info on this in class.

Review

Here are the key items and study points you should review for the quiz:

  • You should be able to define/explain:

  • A definition for GMO/GMO organisms

  • Understand what the Ti Plasmid is and critical things about its design (i.e. restriction enzymes, 35s promotor)
  • An example of how genetic engineering is used to improve food or agriculture
  • Know what gene is an indicator (marker) of potential modification
  • Know what the the purpose of the following steps in our GMO experiment
  • Be able to define what the experimental control was in this experiment and what it tells us.
  • Be able to interpret a simulated (diagram) of a GMO experiment
    electrophoresis and determine
    • If a sample DNA extraction likely worked
    • If a sample was likely GMO
